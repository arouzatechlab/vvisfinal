VIDYA VIHAR INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL – Inspiring Innovation, Nurturing Excellence, Shaping Life-long Learners

Vidya Vihar International School (VVIS), located in India, is a premier educational institution founded with the vision of creating a transformative learning environment that aligns with contemporary global standards. The school is the realization of a shared dream to provide world-class education that empowers every child.

VVIS is managed by the Vidya Vihar Educational Trust, a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education. The school's ethos is rooted in the belief that education should empower individuals to become self-motivated, self-driven, and passionate critical thinkers and responsible citizens.

Vidya Vihar International School is more than just an educational institution; it is a community that fosters intellectual growth, personal development, and a love for lifelong learning. The school's commitment to excellence, innovation, and inclusivity makes it a preferred choice for parents seeking a quality education for their children.
