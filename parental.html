Parental Synergy

Parent-Teacher Partnership

At Vidya Vihar International School, we firmly believe that a strong partnership between parents and teachers is essential for the holistic development of our students. Our commitment to fostering a culture of open communication, mutual respect, and active involvement creates a supportive and enriching learning environment where every child can thrive. Parents are encouraged to actively participate in their child’s educational journey through meaningful activities such as reading together, assisting with homework, discussing school events, attending school functions, and volunteering in classrooms. We deeply value the insights and input of families, recognizing that a strong community is vital for nurturing students’ growth and success.

Communication & Engagement

To facilitate open communication and collaboration, Vidya Vihar International School provides multiple opportunities for regular interaction between parents and teachers. Frequent parent-teacher meetings and structured feedback mechanisms empower parents to share their thoughts and concerns. Parents can meet class teachers and subject teachers on the Second Saturday of every month, enabling meaningful discussions about each child’s strengths, challenges, learning preferences, developmental milestones, and areas for growth. These interactions ensure that parents remain well-informed and actively involved in their child’s educational progress.

Shared Vision & Digital Connect

Regular Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTMs), led by the Principal for each grade, further strengthen this partnership by addressing academic goals, co-curricular activities, innovative programs, and the importance of parental involvement. To enhance communication and engagement, Vidya Vihar International School has developed a dedicated mobile app that provides access to announcements, notifications, event calendars, academic resources, and extracurricular updates. Through strong collaboration between families and the school, we strive to create a stable, encouraging learning environment that supports academic excellence, personal growth, and lifelong success for every child.
